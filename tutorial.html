How to use Kaytrix


The first 2 boxes is for the left matrix' row and column respectively. The middle is the operation. The next two boxes are for the right matrix' row and column sizes. The following buttons are creating the left matrix, the right matrix, and computing both matrices'.


To use Kaytrix:

  1. First enter the matrices' sizes.
  2. Next, select the operation ( + , - , * , / )
  3. After that, you can click on the create matrix button and enter the values for the matrix
  4. Once the values are inputted, press the comput matrix button.
  5. After you compute, you can continue to add, subtract, multiply, and divide the solved matrix with a new matrix until the reset button is pressed, which will reset all matrices to its default values.
  6. You can look at the history for each matrix calculation by clicking the back button. (However, you cannot edit them)
  7. Finally, you can click on the next button to go back to the current state.
